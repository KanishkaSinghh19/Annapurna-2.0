ONLINE DONATIONS TO EMPOWER HOPE IN INDIA
Hotels often discard significant amounts of leftover food, contributing to global food waste. By connecting surplus food to those in need, your platform ensures that edible food is not wasted, aligning with sustainable practices.

The platform can provide a lifeline to underprivileged communities by redistributing surplus food. This helps reduce food insecurity and ensures that nutritious meals reach those who might otherwise go hungry.

About Us

Annapurna is a heartfelt initiative dedicated to eradicating hunger by reducing food wastage and redistributing surplus food to those in need. By collecting leftover food from weddings, restaurants, and events, we ensure it reaches shelters, NGOs, and underserved communities, giving it a second purpose.

Healthy Meals for Families

Promoting health and well-being by providing rescued, nutritious meals to families facing hardships. These meals alleviate financial burdens and offer a sense of security during challenging times.

Seeds of Hope

Delivering wholesome, surplus food to children and youth in underserved areas. By ensuring access to nutritious meals, we empower the next generation to thrive and build a brighter future.

Start with Energy

Providing wholesome, rescued meals to combat hunger and support well-being. These meals ensure individuals stay nourished and energized, empowering them to lead healthier and more productive lives.
